Job Description
Client Support Specialist

This role focuses on building B2B experience in a media software company. Perfect for a recent college grad looking to kick start their career mastering our proprietary software. As a Client Support Specialist, you will focus on the accounting and financial functions of the software. You will report to the Support Manager for Production and Corporate Accounting and provide application support for our clients by identifying, researching, troubleshooting and resolving production and accounting questions and issues.

Key Responsibilities
  • Responsible for developing a team of high performance QA engineers
  • Provide hands-on coaching to all members of cross-functional teams on testing & quality practices
  • Establish visibility into the state of the quality of products including test case coverage, automation and defect discovery throughout the software development lifecycle
  • Communicate test requirements to product management and development teams reviewing project requirements for testability
  • Establish practices and maintain an end-to-end integrated test environment available to all development teams
  • Develop, maintain, and enforce standards for QA standards, practices, and automation
  • Champion a culture of continued learning and improvement
Requirements and Skills
  • BA/BS in Computer Science, a related field or equivalent work experience
  • 3 - 5 years direct QA/Customer Support experience
  • Experience in technical testing using Agile practices
  • Direct experience helping teams adopt Acceptance Test Driven Development or similar approaches (Requirements By Example or Behavior Driven Development)
  • Hands-on experience with test automation tools
  • Demonstrated success in both manual and automated testing
  • Understanding of SaaS, web services, ORM, XML and web applications
  • Experience coaching others including task based mentoring and one-on-one coaching, especially entry-level employees
